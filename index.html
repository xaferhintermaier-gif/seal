The Science
☀️ Morning sunlight
Sunlight within 30 min of waking triggers cortisol release, setting your circadian anchor.
☕ Caffeine half-life
Caffeine has a 5–7 hour half-life. A coffee at 3pm still has half its effect at 10pm.
🍽️ Meal timing
Eating within 3 hours of bed raises core temperature and suppresses melatonin.
📵 Blue light
Screens before bed suppress melatonin by up to 90 minutes. Use night mode 2h before bed.
